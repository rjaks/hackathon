M50 Hobbyist Cafe

ContactReview



Magsaysay Avenue, Naga City

12PM - 8PM, Tuesdays to Sundays

4.5 Rating

Welcome to "M50 Café," a cozy and charming spot where vintage vinyl records and retro treasures come together with exceptional coffee and beverages. This quaint café is a haven for those who love the nostalgic charm of vinyl, featuring a carefully curated collection of records on the walls and a warm, inviting atmosphere. Sip your expertly brewed coffee or artisanal tea, relax on vintage-inspired furniture, and browse through a delightful selection of vintage items, all in the heart of the city. M50 Café is where nostalgia meets your daily caffeine fix, creating a unique and comforting experience for all.
